About Me

Bridging Linguistics & Technology

I'm a PhD student at West University of Timișoara, specializing in corpus linguistics with a focus on telecinematic discourse. My work combines computational methods with linguistic analysis to understand how language functions in contemporary media.

Applied Corpus Linguistics

Building and analyzing specialized corpora to uncover patterns in language use, from academic writing to workplace sitcoms.

Telecinematic Stylistics

Investigating the linguistic features of television and film, with a focus on workplace discourse and pop culture.

Data-Driven Learning

Developing corpus-based tools and pedagogical approaches to enhance language learning and literacy.

Digital Humanities

Creating digital tools and platforms that make linguistic research accessible to broader audiences.

Research Projects

PhD Research: Workplace Sitcoms Corpus

2022 - Present

"Get to Work!" – A comprehensive corpus-based inquiry into the language of contemporary American workplace sitcoms. This dissertation explores how workplace discourse is represented and stylized in popular television, examining patterns of humor, professional interaction, and character development through computational linguistics methods.

corpus linguistics discourse analysis telecinematic studies pop culture

LEMI: Romanian Readability Platform

2023 - 2024

Development of the first corpus-based readability assessment tool for Romanian children's literature. LEMI helps educators and parents select age-appropriate texts and supports literacy development through computational analysis of text complexity.

NLP education technology literacy Romanian language

ROGER Corpus Platform

2020 - 2022

Bilingual corpus of Romanian academic genres providing open-access tools for studying academic writing. Contributed to platform development, corpus annotation, and data analysis as research assistant on this Swiss National Science Foundation project.

academic writing corpus platform bilingual research
